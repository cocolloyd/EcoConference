Full Weekend Package

Guests will attend both Saturday and Sunday's conferences. A continental breakfast, lunch, and dinner will be provided. Registration passes and free gifts will be provided upon arrival. Please contact the office for any dietary restrictions or requests.

Meet with any of our speakers or keynotes before and after the conference days. Guests with this pass will also have access to our special lounge.

Gala After Hours

Enjoy the last day of the conference weekend with a reception, award night, and evening of fun. Celebrate the earth while enjoying food delivered from sustainable companies.
