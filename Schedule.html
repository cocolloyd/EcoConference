FAQs

What is a creative workshop?

A creative workshop is a quick, half-hour side event where lead designers from a wide variety of organizations and countries come to teach a fun skill. Some of the designers set up their own challenges and activities. Try one and see what you can learn! View a full list of our attendees here

What is a design wave?

Design waves are unique to our conference. It aligns with our central theme. Designers and individuals are given a challenge, topic, or question, and they have 30 minutes to come up with whatever they like. Results and ideas are shared on the last day at the gala.

I only want to attend one day of the conference, is that possible?

Absolutely! Make sure to select the one day conference package for your prefered day. The weekend is how you want to make it!
